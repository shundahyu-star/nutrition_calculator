This calculator is a nutrition engine that converts your age, weight, and height into a daily calorie and macro target using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula. It filters a database of over 30 recipes and 60 ingredients to match your specific dietary preferences, such as vegan or vegetarian. 
First, the script uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is the energy your body burns at rest. It then multiplies this by an Activity Factor (1.2 to 1.725) to find your TDEE.
Next, the code applies a "multiplier" to your TDEE based on your goal: it subtracts 20% for a "Cut" (fat loss) or adds 20% for a "Bulk" (muscle gain). It then splits these calories into Macros: Protein is calculated at 2.2g per kg of your body weight, Fats are set to 25% of your total calories, and Carbs fill in whatever energy is left over.
The script scans a database of 30 recipes and filters them based on your dietary restriction (Standard, Vegetarian, or Vegan). It then uses the random.choice() function to pick one breakfast, one lunch, and one dinner.
It compares the calories in a normal version of a meal to your specific personal needs. It calculates a Scale Factor (User's Target Calories ÷ Recipe's Base Calories) and multiplies the gram weight of every raw ingredient by that number, making the meal sized for your body.
The code loops through the scaled ingredients and prints a clean summary.
